Where It All began

SharonKenya started as a campus hobby.
The company's CEO did not realize it by then,
but her way of naturally looking good
even without trying caught his friends' attention.

She would dress contestants of Mr and Miss University and all the judges
gave a lot of credit to her work. Her comments and suggestions on how a
person ought to have chosen an outfit automatically and this was the time her
friend started considering styling as her talent.

Later on while in campus, a feeling of being
trapped in the wrong course hung around her
and would simply not go away. She was torn between
quitting her Buchelor's degree in Business Information

Technology and signing up for a more fulfilling course.
She felt like a designer course was more appropriate for
her. However, she brushed the thought off and pursued her BBIT degree.
Upon graduation, it was as if chains had been lifted off her shoulders
and that is when she registered her company.
